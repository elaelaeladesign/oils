Essential Oils – doTERRA Guide

These small essential oil bottles are your travel-sized companions — only 2ml each, but still powerful in every drop. Because they’re so concentrated, remember: one tap on the bottom of the bottle equals about half a drop.

For internal use, add 1 drop to a large glass of water, or 2–3 drops per liter if you’re mixing a bottle.

For a simple aromatic boost, create your own “aroma bowl”: put 1–2 drops in your hands, rub them together gently, cup your hands over your face, and breathe in deeply. Hold for 10–15 seconds, then exhale slowly — feel the oils ground and uplift you.

Wild Orange

Main Effects:

  • Energizing and uplifting: Promotes positivity, motivation, and balance.
  • Cleansing and purifying: Supports natural detox and purifies air and surfaces.
  • Immune support: Contains antioxidants (limonene) that strengthen the immune system.
  • Soothing and relaxing: Reduces stress while maintaining energy.

Common Uses:

  • Aromatically: Diffuse or inhale directly for a mood boost.
  • Topically: Apply diluted to wrists or temples for freshness and focus.
  • Internally: Add 1–2 drops to water or food for flavor and detox support.
  • Household: Mix with water to clean surfaces and freshen air.

Caution:

  • Avoid sunlight or UV for 12h after topical use (photosensitive).
  • Always dilute before applying to skin.
